About Hipobuy Spreadsheet

Hipobuy Spreadsheet is built as a buyer-side workspace: a place to organize find routes, QC questions, sizing risks, and parcel decisions before shipping.

Why it exists

Most buyers do not need more random links. They need a process for deciding which links are worth submitting and which items are safe enough to ship.
